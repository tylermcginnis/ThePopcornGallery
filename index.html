Frequently Asked Questions

When will The Popcorn Gallery be Available?
As soon as possible. If you have an iPhone, that means April 1st.
Where did the name come from?
It's a reference to the peanut gallery back in Shakespeare's time. Popcorn instead of peanuts, in reference to modern film.
I'm interested in being a BETA tester, how can I make that happen?
Hit up the "Sign Up" page below. Your chances go up if you fill it out hundreds of times. Wait. That's not true.
Have you thought about _____ as a feature for The Popcorn Gallery?
Yes. Or maybe. Sometimes No? We love to hear suggestions from our users. We're building this for you, so let us know what you want to see on twitter @thepopcornteam.
Sounds great. How much is this going to cost me?
Nothing. We promise. The Popcorn Gallery will always be free to users.
Is this going to spam my Facebook friends or Twitter stream?
Nope. We'd love for you to let your friends know all about us, but we're not interested in posting anything to your wall/stream.
